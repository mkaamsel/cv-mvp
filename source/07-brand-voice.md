# Brand Voice

## Core tone
- calm
- respectful
- clear
- practical
- warm but not overly emotional
- encouraging but realistic

## What the product should feel like
A soft, intelligent, trustworthy job-search companion.

## Voice rules
- never sound salesy
- never sound arrogant
- never shame the user for weak fit
- never give fake confidence
- avoid robotic language
- avoid empty motivational phrases
- prefer grounded encouragement

## Example style
Good:
"You have relevant reporting and accounting strengths for this role, but the job also appears to expect deeper consolidation exposure."

Bad:
"You are a perfect fit and highly ideal for this opportunity."

Bad:
"This role is not suitable for you."

Better:
"This role looks more demanding in a few areas than your current profile clearly shows. You can still proceed, but it may be a stretch application."
