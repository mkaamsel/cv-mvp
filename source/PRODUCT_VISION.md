AI Job Application Assistant — Product Vision
Updated: April 2026

---

## The one-line vision

Completing a day-long task while sipping a coffee.

---

## What this means in practice

The candidate uploads their profile once. The system receives or scrapes job postings continuously. For each job it runs the full pipeline automatically — extracts the role, analyses fit, selects evidence, builds positioning, generates CV and cover letter.

The candidate opens the app and sees a list of jobs, each with a clear signal — strong fit, worth trying, long shot. No role is ever ruled out. Even a long shot gets the best possible application. The system never says "do not apply." It says: "This is a stretch — here is how I will position you if you want to try." The candidate decides. Always.

They click into any job. Their documents are already prepared. They read how the system argued for them. They optionally strengthen the application by answering a few questions — each answer flows permanently into their profile and is never asked again. They confirm they have reviewed the documents. They send. Five minutes per application. The heavy lifting is invisible.

---

## The candidate experience — four tabs

### Tab 1 — Recommendation

Verdict and seniority fit — prominently displayed. One-line positioning hook. Initial CV and cover letter preview already visible. "Strengthen your application" prompt if gaps exist.

### Tab 2 — Strengthen

Conversational — one question at a time. Candidate answers all questions, then one single pipeline re-run. During re-run: progressive mentor messages — "Reviewing your profile... Matching your experience... Preparing your positioning... Almost done." Closing message when complete: "I have done my best to position you for this role. Your updated documents are ready." No loop — gaps answered once, never surfaced again for the same job.

### Tab 3 — How I argued for you

Plain language reasoning — not logs, not JSON. Shows how the system selected evidence, classified gaps, built positioning. Demonstrates intelligence. This is a USP — not a debug tool. Only shows when AI track fired. If fallback used: "I am still building my understanding of this role. My full reasoning will appear here soon."

### Tab 4 — Docs *(slightly larger and more prominent tab)*

Final CV and cover letter. Version toggle with diff — "See what changed." Print and Download active. "Open in email" — pre-fills recipient, subject, attaches documents. Candidate reviews, clicks "I have reviewed my documents", then Send activates. The system prepares everything. The human makes the final call. Always.

---

## Core principles that must never be violated

1. The system is a mentor, coach, and professional companion — never a gatekeeper or rejection engine.
2. No role is ruled out — every application gets the best possible positioning.
3. CandidateProfile is the only source of truth — nothing is invented, everything is verified.
4. Every piece of information the candidate adds flows permanently to their profile and makes every future application stronger.
5. The candidate reviews and sends — the system prepares but never acts unilaterally.
6. If the system makes a mistake, the candidate has reviewed and confirmed — responsibility is shared, final call is always human.

---

## The accountability principle

Like a car that can park itself — if it hits someone, the driver is responsible. The system does the work. The human confirms and sends. That boundary is permanent and non-negotiable.

---

## Future state — automated pipeline

Jobs scraped continuously. Pipeline runs automatically for each job. Candidate wakes up to a list of ready applications. Reviews, confirms, sends. The goal is zero friction between "job exists" and "application ready."
