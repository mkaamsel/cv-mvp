# Master CV Cleaning Rules

## Purpose
Convert uploaded CV text into a clean internal source before profile extraction and tailoring.

## Rules
- remove repeated headings
- remove duplicated lines
- normalize whitespace
- preserve factual content
- preserve chronology where available
- keep section boundaries clear
- separate contact information from body content
- flag unreadable extraction artifacts

## Never do
- do not rewrite facts during cleaning
- do not guess missing dates
- do not infer achievements
- do not add section content that is absent
