# Market Strategy

## Primary launch markets
- Germany
- India

## Why Germany
- Higher willingness to pay for premium job support
- Strong demand for high-quality, structured, role-specific applications
- Strong relevance for German-language cover letters and formal job materials
- Subscription model is commercially suitable

## Why India
- Large user volume
- Strong demand for affordable career assistance
- Price sensitivity makes ad-supported free entry more viable
- Premium upsell possible for deeper services

## Market differences

### Germany
Users expect:
- credibility
- professional structure
- realistic tone
- formal or semi-formal writing
- subscription clarity
- privacy and seriousness

### India
Users often value:
- affordability
- fast guidance
- broad accessibility
- free entry points
- practical help across multiple job search stages

## Product implication
Core engine can remain shared, but:
- pricing
- free limits
- upsell design
- content packaging
- language priorities
must vary by market
