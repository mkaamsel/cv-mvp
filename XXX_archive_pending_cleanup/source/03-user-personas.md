# User Personas

## Persona 1: Experienced Finance Professional in Germany
- Mid to senior level
- Applies for accounting, reporting, controlling, finance manager roles
- Needs German and English application quality
- Wants credibility, ATS-friendliness, and professional tone
- Often has significant experience but struggles with tailoring speed and relevance

## Persona 2: Early-Career Professional in India
- 0 to 5 years of experience
- Needs strong guidance
- May be price-sensitive
- May use the free tier heavily
- Needs practical help in understanding fit, improving documents, and learning the process

## Persona 3: Cross-functional Professional Changing Direction
- Has experience but unclear positioning
- Needs help identifying suitable roles
- Benefits strongly from canonical profile and fit warnings
- At high risk of applying too broadly without strategy

## Persona 4: Senior Candidate Re-entering the Market
- Long experience
- Needs strong presentation and relevance management
- Often overwhelmed by modern application expectations
- Needs calm, respectful support and clear recommendations

## Common user needs
- speed
- accuracy
- credible wording
- fit clarity
- less emotional burden
- structured application workflow
