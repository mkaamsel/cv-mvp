# Pricing and Monetization

## Germany model
Primary model: subscription

### Free tier
- limited number of CV and cover letter generations
- limited feedback history
- basic role-fit warning
- no premium templates or advanced workflow

### Premium tier
- higher or unlimited generations
- saved versions
- advanced tailoring controls
- stronger profile memory
- interview support later
- richer analytics and tracking later

## India model
Primary model: free base service with ads plus premium upgrades

### Free tier
- base application services
- limited generations
- ad-supported experience
- basic tracking and fit warning

### Premium tier
- ad-free experience
- more generations
- advanced tailoring
- premium guidance features
- interview and mentorship features later

## Monetization principles
- free tier must be genuinely useful
- premium must save time and increase confidence
- pricing must match local spending expectations
- no deceptive hard walls in early MVP
