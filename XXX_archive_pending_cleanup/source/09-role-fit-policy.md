# Role-Fit Policy

## Purpose
Role-fit exists to guide the user, not block them aggressively.

## MVP behavior
- show fit score
- show key strengths
- show key gaps
- show a warning banner when mismatch seems meaningful
- user may still continue

## Warning conditions
A warning should appear when:
- critical required experience is clearly missing
- the role appears substantially more senior
- the field is materially different
- required tools or frameworks are central and absent
- leadership expectations exceed available evidence

## Tone rules
- avoid harsh rejection language
- explain the warning
- keep user agency

## Future direction
Later the system may classify roles as:
- strong fit
- reasonable stretch
- weak fit
- not recommended
