# Product Vision

## Why this product exists

Job search is one of the most stressful, repetitive, and emotionally draining experiences in a professional life. Candidates are expected to understand their strengths, identify suitable roles, tailor applications, track progress, prepare for interviews, and make career decisions — often while unemployed, under pressure, and without structured support.

Most existing tools solve only one narrow part of this journey, such as job search, resume optimization, cover letter generation, or application tracking. This forces users to stitch together multiple tools and still do the strategic thinking and emotional management alone.

This product exists to simplify, humanize, and strengthen the full job application journey.

## Vision

Build an AI job search companion that acts as a calm, practical, and trustworthy assistant across the candidate lifecycle.

The product should function as:
- a mentor
- a strategist
- a writing assistant
- an application organizer
- an interview support system
- a soft, respectful companion during a difficult professional period

## Long-term ambition

The long-term ambition is to support the complete journey across four phases:
1. Preparation
2. Application
3. Interview preparation
4. Offer and career decision support

## What makes this product different

This is not only a CV upload and output generator.

It is designed to become a comprehensive system that:
- understands the candidate deeply
- builds a canonical candidate profile
- identifies suitable roles
- tailors CVs and cover letters credibly
- warns against weak-fit applications
- tracks applications and follow-ups
- supports interview preparation
- helps users close skill gaps over time

## Product philosophy

The system must always prioritize:
- credibility over polish
- usefulness over verbosity
- guidance over spam automation
- support over pressure
- realism over false encouragement

## Target users

This is a multi-user SaaS for professionals across different:
- seniority levels
- job families
- industries
- markets

Initial primary markets:
- Germany
- India

## Business direction

Germany:
- subscription-led model
- limited free access
- premium workflow and guidance features

India:
- free base access supported by ads
- optional paid premium services
- price-sensitive but high-volume market strategy

## Success definition

The product succeeds if users:
- apply faster
- apply with more confidence
- produce better applications
- avoid poor-fit roles
- receive more interview invitations
- feel less alone and less overwhelmed during job search
